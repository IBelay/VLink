Company Overview Created for the purpose of making the job application and job hunting process more interactive and result-oriented. VidSki is formed to include all segment in the job application process such as employers, job seekers, talent solution and even schools to help network and engage better to link to the right person and/or organization. By using VidSki, users will minimize their search process and focus on what is more important: the content of their application.

Profile

VidSki was formed due to the need for a completely dedicated site to our need as students and see results from rigorously searching for job or trying to get the attention of hiring managers. We plan to explore the different possibilities to make the process more content oriented and less stress-free process.

Mission

Our mission is to help undiscovered talents and creativity to be explored via our video tools and sourcing platform. On VidSki, we want to help both job seekers and employers explore the possibilities and skills required and go beyond the surface of searching for the right keyword on the resume.

Vision

Our ultimate goal is to make it a smooth and stress-free journey for all targeted segments. Our strategy is to use the video medium to maximize the reach and not just volume. With the appealing video, viewers can get the information needed without overwhelming them with the reading of long text applications.
